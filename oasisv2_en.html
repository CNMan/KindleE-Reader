Version 5.13.1 – June 2020

Here’s what's new:

New features for kids in Amazon FreeTime: Kids will see a new layout, improved search, book details, and Audible support. In supported marketplaces, kids with access to Amazon FreeTime Unlimited will see rows of recommended eBook and Audible content that are age-appropriate.

Search: We have updated the search experience making it easier to navigate through your search results in the book without losing the page you were reading. To get started, simply search for a term in your book and tap on a search result.

Dark Mode: We have made it easier for you to turn on Dark Mode. You can easily turn it on/off in Quick Actions. Available on 2017 and later Kindle Oasis and Paperwhite.

Updated Page Flip Navigation: We have updated the Page Flip experience that shows recently read pages as dots on the progress bar, including your most recent page read from another device.

Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.
