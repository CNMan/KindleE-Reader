Your Kindle is regularly updated with improvements to the reading experience. Have feedback? Please share it with the Kindle team by going to Contact Us under Settings and then Help & User Guides

Version 5.16.9 – May 2024

Here's what's new:

Notebook Per Page Templates: You can change Notebook templates for individual, selected, or all pages in a notebook. Change the template in Notebook Settings or Notebook Overview.

Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.

Version 5.16.8 – April 2024

Sleep Timer: Set how long your Kindle waits before going to the lock screen from Settings > Device Options.

Library Search Filter: Search results can now be filtered by subscription type and reading format.

Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.
