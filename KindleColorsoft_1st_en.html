Your Kindle is regularly updated with improvements to the reading experience. Have feedback? Please share it with the Kindle team by going to Contact Us under Settings and then Help & User Guides

Version 5.18.2 – April 2025

Here's what's new:

Author name appears First Last.: Author names now appear in the format of First Last (previously Last, First) in Library. The sort order will still be based on the order last name.

Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.

Version 5.18.1 – March 2025

Recaps for Books in Series: You can now access short recaps for thousands of best-selling English language Kindle books in series you have purchased or borrowed. Refresh your memory on what happened in a previously read book prior to starting a new book in series.

Double Tap to Page Turn: You can now double tap on the sides or back of your device to turn pages in books or scroll down in Home/Library. This feature can be turned on or off in Settings>Device Options. Available on Signature Edition only.

Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.
