Performance improvements, bug fixes, and other general enhancements.


Version 5.14.1 – October 2021

Page Turn Animation: You can now change the page turn style in most books. The new feature is available under the Aa Menu -> Other, and adds a new page turn style that has a directional animation.

Navigation options while reading: You can now navigate more easily within a book that has footnotes, endnotes, bookmarks, notes/highlights, or links.

Improvements to Device Identification : Kindle Model and Generation information is now discoverable on your Kindle in Settings within the Device Info page.
